Common Questions

What you're
probably wondering

What if AI automation doesn't actually work for my business? +
That's exactly why we start with a free 15-minute discovery call and, if there's a fit, a no-cost workflow audit. If we can't find at least three high-ROI opportunities, we'll tell you honestly — and you walk away with a clearer map of your operations either way. We'd rather say "this isn't the right fit right now" than take on an engagement that won't deliver real results.
How long does a typical implementation take? +
Most initial automations go live within 2–4 weeks after we start. Simpler workflows (like AI-driven scheduling or review automation) can be deployed in days. We intentionally start narrow and fast, then scale, so you see measurable results within your first month — not a year into a massive transformation project.
Do I need a technical team or IT staff to work with you? +
No. We handle the technical implementation end-to-end. You need someone who understands your business and its workflows — that's it. We translate between the tech and the business outcomes, and we document everything so your team is never dependent on us to use what we build.
Is this going to replace my employees? +
In almost every engagement, the answer is no — it frees your team from repetitive, low-value work so they can focus on things that actually grow the business: customer relationships, complex problem-solving, and strategic work. The most common outcome we see is the same team doing dramatically more revenue, not layoffs.
How secure is this? What data will you have access to? +
Security is our founder's background — this is one area where we don't cut corners. We work within your existing systems whenever possible, use enterprise-grade tools with proper data handling, and architect every automation with the principle of least privilege. We'll walk you through exactly what access is needed for each automation and why, before anything goes live.
What does it typically cost? +
Engagements vary based on scope and the tools involved, but most first projects fall in the mid-four-figure to low-five-figure range — and are designed to pay for themselves within 60–90 days. After the free workflow audit, you'll receive a detailed proposal with exact costs and projected ROI so you can make the call with full transparency. No surprises, no retainer traps.
What tools and platforms do you work with? +
We're platform-agnostic and choose tools based on what fits your business, not what pays us a commission. Common building blocks include Make.com, n8n, Zapier, GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Bland AI, Voiceflow, PandaDoc, Birdeye, QuickBooks, and a range of LLM providers. Where you already have systems in place, we integrate with them.
What happens after the 15-minute call? +
If there's a clear fit, we schedule the deeper 45-minute workflow audit (also free). From there, you receive a written automation blueprint with scope, cost, and projected ROI. If you decide to move forward, we build. If not, you keep the blueprint. There's no pressure and no obligation at any step.
